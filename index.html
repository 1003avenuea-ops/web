Sights & Outdoors
Robert Redden Footbridge 👟
A pedestrian bridge lit over the Oostanaula that is worth visiting at any hour.
Myrtle Hill 👟
A historic cemetery with sweeping views of Rome and the rivers below.
City Clocktower 👟
Rome's hilltop landmark with some of the best views in town.
ECO Greenway Trails 👟
Miles of riverside trail for walking, running, and biking right from the neighborhood.
Ridge Ferry Park 👟
Riverside park with trails and open green space along the Oostanaula.
Berry College 🚗
Miles of trails through one of the most spectacular and historically scenic college campuses in the country.
Recreation
Berry Tennis Center 🚗
World-class courts on the Berry College campus, about a 10-minute drive.
Downtown Tennis & Pickleball 👟
Public courts in the heart of Rome. Easy to walk to.
