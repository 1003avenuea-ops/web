Explore Rome

Our personal picks — the places we'd send a friend to on their very first visit.

Sights to See
Robert Redden Footbridge

A pedestrian bridge lit up over the Oostanaula — magical at any hour.

Myrtle Hill Cemetery

One of the most beautiful hilltop cemeteries in the South — sweeping views of Rome and the rivers below.

City Clocktower

Rome's beloved hilltop landmark. A short walk up rewards you with one of the best views in town.

Morning
Swift & Finch Coffee

The best espresso in Rome. Cozy, welcoming, and the perfect way to start your day.

Lunch
Schroeder's New Deli

A Rome institution. Classic sandwiches and deli fare done right — don't miss it.

Dinner & Drinks
Aventine

Upscale Italian in a beautifully restored downtown space. The kind of dinner you'll talk about after.

Harvest Moon Cafe

Warm, relaxed, and consistently great. A local favorite for a reason.

Dark Side of the Moon

Rome's go-to late-night bar. Craft drinks, good energy, great crowd.

Bites & Sweets
Honeymoon Bakery

Exceptional pastries baked fresh daily. Stop in — you won't regret it.

The Sweet Bar

Frozen treats and candy in a fun, lively spot. A must if you have a sweet tooth.

Outdoors
ECO Heritage Trail System

Miles of trails along the river — walkable right from the neighborhood.

Ridge Ferry Park

Beautiful riverside park with trails and open green space. One of Rome's finest.

Recreation
Berry Tennis Center

World-class courts on the Berry College campus — a short drive and absolutely worth it.

Downtown Tennis & Pickleball

Public courts in the heart of Rome. Easy to walk to, great for a quick match.

Essentials
Publix

Your go-to for groceries, snacks, and anything you need during your stay.
